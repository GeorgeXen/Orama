An Unforgettable Erasmus+ Experience in Râmnicu Sărat, Romania!

From September 13 to 22, 2024, participants from over 10 countries gathered in Râmnicu Sărat for an enriching Erasmus+ project organized by Asociația de Tineret ONIX. This unique initiative focused on empowering youth workers through non-formal education, promoting sustainable local development, and fostering intercultural dialogue. Over the course of the week, participants engaged in workshops, field visits, and cultural exchanges, all designed to share best practices and build a more inclusive, green, and collaborative future for youth work.


One of the highlights of the project was the immersive stay at Hecas Villas, where participants connected with nature and each other, strengthening the bonds of friendship and cooperation.


Here’s what some of our participants had to say:


"This project introduced me to so many new cultures and backgrounds, allowing me to form meaningful friendships across the globe. Spending time at Hecas Villas, surrounded by nature, and meeting people from diverse countries was an unforgettable experience. I can’t wait to return to Râmnicu Sărat for the next one!"
— Chris


"Connecting with old friends while making new ones from around the world was the highlight of this project. The workshops were engaging, and visiting the Muddy Volcanoes was one of the many memorable moments. Huge thanks to Florin, Cosmin, and the entire ONIX team for such a well-organized and inspiring event!"
— Christofer


This Erasmus+ program truly exemplifies the power of collaboration, cultural exchange, and shared knowledge. A big thank you to Florin Ceparu, Cosmin Saic, and the ONIX team for making this possible and for building bridges that will last a lifetime.


We look forward to continuing this journey of growth and connection!
