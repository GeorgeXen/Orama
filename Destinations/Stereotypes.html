Join the SRS Nexus Project in Cyprus!

Are you passionate about challenging stereotypes and exploring the impact of role models and social norms? Do you want to be part of a movement that empowers youth workers to foster inclusivity and critical thinking in society? If so, we invite you to join our transformative project: SRS Nexus.

About SRS Nexus

The SRS Nexus (Stereotypes, Role Models, and Social Norms) project is an Erasmus+ initiative designed to empower youth workers and young people by addressing and challenging the stereotypes, role models, and social norms that shape our society. This project brings together participants from various countries to engage in seminars, workshops, and creative activities that foster self-awareness, critical thinking, and empathy. By exploring the impact of social media influencers and societal expectations, the SRS Nexus project aims to promote inclusivity, diversity, and positive role models.

SRS Nexus recognizes the powerful influence that social media and cultural norms have on shaping identities and societal behaviors. Through this project, participants will learn to critically examine the role of influencers and societal expectations, develop a deeper understanding of the biases that affect their perceptions, and explore how to promote more inclusive and realistic representations of success and leadership. The project is a proactive response to the challenges posed by social media culture, offering youth the tools and knowledge needed to navigate these influences constructively.
