Join the Carepreneur Project in Cyprus!

Are you passionate about digital entrepreneurship and the power of emotional intelligence? Do you want to be part of a movement that empowers youth workers to create impactful, empathetic digital businesses? If so, we invite you to join our innovative project: CAREPRENEUR.

About Carepreneur

"CAREPRENEUR" is an Erasmus+ project designed to empower youth workers by enhancing their digital entrepreneurship skills through a unique focus on Digital Emotional Intelligence (DEQ). The project, which includes seminars and workshops, aims to develop participants' digital business plans while integrating key methodologies such as design thinking, empathy mapping, and brand identity. The project's core principle revolves around the importance of digital empathy and self-awareness, linking these attributes directly to successful digital entrepreneurship.

In the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, which has significantly accelerated the digital transformation of workspaces, there has been a growing need for advanced digital skills. However, only a small fraction of the workforce possesses these competencies. Additionally, the pandemic has taken a toll on mental health, particularly among young adults, exacerbating issues like anxiety, depression, and stress. "CAREPRENEUR" addresses these challenges by fostering resilience, self-awareness, and empathy among youth workers, enabling them to navigate the digital world more effectively.

The project brings together participants from diverse cultural backgrounds, encouraging the exchange of ideas and experiences. It emphasizes hands-on learning, with activities designed to enhance participants' digital marketing skills, communication techniques, and overall entrepreneurial competencies. By the end of the project, participants will have gained the skills necessary to build and manage digital businesses while maintaining empathetic and self-aware relationships in the online space.

Why Join Carepreneur?

Enhance Your Digital Skills: Gain hands-on experience in creating digital business plans, developing a brand identity, and learning effective digital marketing strategies.

Develop Emotional Intelligence: Improve your ability to empathize with others online and manage your digital relationships effectively, which is crucial in today's digital economy.

Improve Self-Awareness: Through various activities and workshops, you will gain a deeper understanding of your values, strengths, and how to leverage them in the digital space.

Network with International Peers: Collaborate with youth workers from different countries, exchange ideas, and learn from diverse perspectives.

Contribute to Social Inclusion: The project also focuses on social inclusion, ensuring that all participants, including those with fewer opportunities, can benefit from the program.
