Join the Green fashiON Project in Latvia!

Are you passionate about sustainable fashion and living a minimalist lifestyle? Do you want to be part of a movement that promotes eco-friendly practices and reduces environmental impact? If so, we invite you to join our exciting new project in Latvia: Green fashiON.

About Green fashiON

Green fashiON is dedicated to transforming the fashion industry by promoting sustainable practices and encouraging a minimal lifestyle. Our mission is to create a community that values quality over quantity, embraces eco-friendly choices, and supports local artisans and sustainable brands.

We believe that small changes in our daily lives can lead to significant positive impacts on the environment. By choosing sustainable fashion and adopting a minimalist approach, we can reduce waste, lower our carbon footprint, and promote a healthier planet for future generations.

Why Join Green fashiON?

Be Part of a Community: Connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for sustainability and minimalism.

Educational Workshops: Attend workshops and seminars on sustainable fashion, upcycling, and minimal living.

Exclusive Events: Participate in exclusive events such as eco-fashion shows, swap meets, and DIY sessions.

Support Local Artisans: Discover and support local artisans and sustainable brands in Latvia.

Make a Difference: Contribute to a movement that aims to make the world a better place through conscious living.

How to Get Involved

We are looking for enthusiastic individuals who are interested in participating in our project. Whether you are a fashion designer, a sustainability advocate, or simply someone who wants to learn more about sustainable living, we welcome you to join us.

To express your interest in participating in the Green fashiON project, please fill out the form below. We look forward to welcoming you to our community and working together to promote sustainable fashion and a minimal lifestyle in Latvia.
